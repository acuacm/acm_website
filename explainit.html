Frequently Asked

When and where do you meet?
We meet on Tuesdays at 7pm in MBB 316.

Is ACM for CS majors only?
ACM is for anyone interested in computers, software, design, or graphics. All majors are invited.

Do I need to have previous experience to participate?
No previous knowledge required. ACM will teach you whatever you need to know, and if you would like additional learning you can ask around, someone will help.

When are officer elections?
The first meeting of each spring semester.

Hardware

Why do gamers prefer wired mice?
Wireless are useful for travelling and keeping a desk clean, but when a few seconds determines life or death in a game, they won't do. Wired mice have no delay and don't need batteries replaced.
