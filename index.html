Welcome to ACM

We are a student-run organization dedicated to offering students extra-curricular activities in the computer science field.

We work on programming projects, offer crash courses for a variety of languages, hold programming contests, and invite guest speakers from the industry to tell us a little about what they do. A few times during the semester we also have LAN parties, usually with food. Check the calendar for all the events.

Join us in MBB 316 on Thursdays from 4:30pm to 5:30pm.

Minerva Project

Minerva Project is a space shooter game that is being flag-shipped by DET club (they do animation and digital media). ACM will be doing the programming for the project and DET will provide the 3D models and so forth.

The project is being built in Unity and the languages available are C# and javascript. We are currently leaning toward javascript but that is to be decided.

ACM may have a crash course on a Tuesday for members who are less familiar with coding in general, the date is to be decided.
